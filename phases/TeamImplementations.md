# Team Implementations

## Teams

- Staffs
- Admins/Managers
- Developers

## Tasks for each teams

### Staffs

- Help collect data of each cinema
  - Informations about each hall
  - Seating plan of each hall

> To collect data that is needed by the cinema service.

### Admins/Managers

- Define reports types needed to show
- Provide business logic needed for the application

### Developers

Developers will be divided into **2 teams**, each team has **3 to 4 developers**.

- Phase I
  - Estimated Timeline: 6 ~ 8 months
  - Teams Division:
      - Team 1 : Auth, Cinema Service, Admin Portal, Reporting Service
      - Team 2 : Ticket Service, Payment Service, POS Machines

- Phase II
  - Estimated Timeline: 4 ~ 6 months
  - Teams Division:
      - Team 1 : Mobile Apps (iOS, Android)
      - Team 2 : Kiosk Machines, Web Application
